About Us

Hi! Our names are Zaydaan and Sohum, and we're both TKS Foundations Students.

Ever since we've been introduced to space travel, we were looking for ways to revolutionize the industry. We were trying to find places to get started, when we stumbled upon ion propulsion.

WE'RE REVOLUTIONIZING THE SPACE INDUSTRY

Our current space travel relies on many resources, is very expensive, and can't go that far.

The concept of ion propulsion uses positively charged atoms to be powered. This saves resources, money, and can go very, very far.

Along with using our ion propulsion-powered spaceships, we will be using LiDAR for docking. On top of that, we will make our spaceships autonomous for a more relaxing experience.
